# 03 - Correct A2A Positioning

## Priority

Wave 1: fix false claims.

## Problem

The current framing overstates the death of A2A. A2A is not dead; it is alive in enterprise contexts but has weaker developer adoption.

## Task

Revise the A2A language and figure caption.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Replace "dead" or equivalent wording with "enterprise-niche" or "alive but niche".
- Distinguish enterprise adoption from developer adoption.
- The revised text remains critical without overstating the claim.
